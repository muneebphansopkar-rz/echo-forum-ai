Just launched: Quillr — an AI writing sidekick that lives in your menubar
PR
priya_r OP · RZ Team · 3h ago · edited 2h ago

After 6 months of building in public, Quillr is live on Product Hunt today. It's a native menubar app for macOS that plugs into any text field and helps you rephrase, expand, or summarise content on demand — no copy-paste, no tab switching.

Would love feedback from this community especially on:

1. Onboarding flow — is 3 steps too many for a menubar app?
2. Pricing — $9/mo solo, $19/mo team. Does it feel right?
3. The label "Rephrase" — should it be "Rewrite"?

PH link in comments. Thanks for all the support so far! 🚀
